    Psychosense V2.0

    PsychoSense V2.0 is an advanced application designed for analyzing and visualizing sentiment and emotional states from various data sources, including text journal entries, audio logs, and video recordings. The application leverages natural language processing, machine learning, computer vision, and speech recognition technologies to provide insights into users' mental and emotional health. It offers a comprehensive platform for users to track their emotional well-being over time and visualize their data in meaningful ways..
